Why Somnia?

Somnia, a cozy place to recreate your fantasies

Founded 15 days before a global pandemic. Daniel Arevalo and Jota the Magician fuse the art of cocktails and magic to create a unique place in the bowels of Barcelona.

Somnia is a signature cocktail bar set in a twentieth century fair with nods to the circus of the same era. We immerse the client in a magical atmosphere created by the decoration of the place, the spectacular cocktails, both the service and the taste. And of course, the magic show table by table every day. The goal is to make Somnia a reference point for cocktails and illusionism in Barcelona.

Hospitality, comfort and the wow effect for our customers are our number one priority.
